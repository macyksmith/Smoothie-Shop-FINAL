Our Story

Smooth Vibes was built for students who want something fast, healthy, and customizable — without guessing what fits their goals. Our pilot adds an AI Concierge that recommends real menu items based on dietary needs and preferences.

What we’re building

A smoothie shop website that feels like a real product: menu → cart → checkout (pilot), plus decision support through a grounded AI assistant.

  • Speed: pickup-first ordering flow designed to reduce lines.
  • Clarity: dietary tags and allergen-aware recommendations.
  • Customization: a builder that outputs a clean, readable order summary.
  • Trust: the concierge uses our menu + policies (not made-up info).

Who it’s for (target users)

  • Students who want high-protein or low-sugar choices quickly.
  • Customers with dairy-free needs who want safe substitutions.
  • Anyone who wants “tell me what to order” guidance without scrolling.

Pilot success metric: % of visitors who use Concierge → add to cart → submit checkout.

Why GenAI belongs here

The “menu decision” problem is real: people have different goals (energy, protein, low-sugar), allergies, and taste preferences. A plain menu makes users do the work.

  • AI turns vague intent (“not too sweet, dairy-free”) into a concrete order.
  • It reduces friction and increases conversion in a pickup-first business.
  • It can adapt responses to each user, while staying grounded in store data.

How the AI stays grounded

In the pilot, the Concierge is built on a structured knowledge base: menu items, tags, allergens, and store policies.

Knowledge Base (pilot) - menu items (name, price, calories, protein, tags, allergens) - store hours + pickup policy - substitution rules + cross-contact disclaimer Retrieval (pilot) - keyword overlap + tag matching - recommendations filtered to real menu items Production path - connect UI to serverless GenAI endpoint + vector search (RAG) - log feedback + improve prompts + update knowledge base

Pilot plan (stakeholder readiness)

  • Stakeholders: 10–20 students testing ordering flow + Concierge helpfulness.
  • What we measure: chat usage, add-to-cart rate, checkout completion, feedback scores.
  • Iteration loop: update menu/policies → refine prompts/retrieval → re-test weekly.
Next: add analytics + a feedback form and connect the concierge to a real GenAI endpoint.
